# OpenMic

**Open-source meeting capture for macOS. No bots. No lock-in. Your notes, your way.**

OpenMic captures meeting audio directly from your Mac's system audio — no bot joins your call. Notes are stored as plain markdown files on disk. Built for an agent-first world where your AI tools need direct access to your meeting knowledge.

## Why OpenMic?

- **No bot joining calls** — Captures system audio via macOS Core Audio tap. Works with Zoom, Meet, Teams, Slack Huddles, or any app that outputs audio.
- **Markdown-first** — Notes stored as `.md` files in a directory you control. Git-friendly. Grep-friendly. Agent-friendly.
- **Local-first** — Audio processing and transcription happen on-device by default. Your meetings never leave your Mac unless you want them to.
- **Open API** — REST API for programmatic access to notes, transcripts, and metadata. No MCP-only lock-in.
- **Agent-readable by default** — Structured frontmatter, consistent formatting, and file-based storage mean any AI tool can read your notes without special integrations.
